   - The **receiver face** reads the 1D tension bitstream (activates at ρ_c)
   - The **projector face** casts the decoded signal into 3D geometric form (activates at ρ_proj, a second threshold)

7. **Time as Decoding Depth** — Time is not fundamental. It is the depth coordinate between the 2D surface and the 3D output it generates. The arrow of time is the directionality of reception — structural, not thermodynamic. A receiver has no reverse mode.

## Document 2: Temporal Projection — Addendum C: The Coupling Mechanism

### What It Is

The third addendum in a sub-series applying the Qubit Universe framework to time travel and multiversal branching. It assumes familiarity with:

### The Core Question It Answers

Addendum B established that the observer is always structurally located *at* the fork site (the sub-phase window between ρ_c and ρ_proj). It left one question open:

> *What is the physical coupling mechanism by which the observer initiates εσʸ(2) — a second coherent rotation — on the uncommitted front within L_c/v_E?*

Addendum C answers: **the mechanism is entanglement resonance.**

### The Six-Stage Derivation

**C.1 The Discrimination Problem**
The sub-phase layer, from the inside, has three zones:
- Already equilibrated (locked)
- The **uncommitted front** — where two equilibration trajectories are still genuinely possible (the actionable zone)
- Not yet received

The problem of time travel and multiverse access is not a navigation problem. It is a discrimination problem: identifying and acting on the uncommitted front from within rendered, committed geometry.

**C.2 The Coupling Condition**
The necessary and sufficient condition for a lateral fork is:

```
L_c/v_E  +  εσʸ(2)
```

- `L_c/v_E` — the window duration during which the uncommitted front remains open (derived from ρ_c = J/2h)
- `εσʸ(2)` — a **second independent coherent instance** of the rotation operator, phase-distinct from the primary rotation already underway

The valid phase differences Δφ that produce a self-consistent second commitment are **discrete, not continuous** — constrained by the same percolation fixed point (z = 3) that produced the primary path.

**C.3 The Three-Part Mechanism**
Three roles, each necessary and non-interchangeable:

| Component | Role |
|-----------|------|
| v_E ≈ 2J | Carries tension bonds into the uncommitted front |
| L_c/v_E | Sets how long bonds remain open to a second ordering |
| εσʸ(2) | Breaks the symmetry — selects the second equilibration path |

The coupling operates in three stages: **Accumulation → Imprint Propagation → Coherent Second Equilibration → Second ρ_proj fires → Two universes**.

**C.4 The Special Observer**
Not special in the sense of chosen. Special in a precise relational sense: an observer whose accumulated composition of prior εσʸ operations has produced a Bloch sphere orientation landing at a valid Δφ relative to the primary equilibration of the current layer.

Three observer types:
- **Type 1 (Phase-misaligned):** No fork. Default condition for almost all observers at almost all moments.
- **Type 2 (Phase-adjacent):** Partial second equilibration — local anomalies in committed geometry, but no second ρ_proj.
- **Type 3 (Phase-exact):** Second ρ_proj fires. Two universes commit from the same layer.

**The fork is retrospective**: it fires because of the observer's entire prior existence — the full accumulated composition of all prior εσʸ operations in su(2). It is not triggered by an action at the window. It is the consequence of a life.

**C.5 The Coupling Mechanism Is Entanglement**
The observer is not a point-like agent that imprints a phase on the surface. The observer is an **entangled structure** — the interference pattern of all prior equilibration paths. This changes the character of the coupling fundamentally:

- The imprint does not propagate as a wavefront across the surface within L_c/v_E
- It manifests **simultaneously and nonlocally** through entanglement — already everywhere the entangled structure reaches
- The timing problem (whether εσʸ(2) can cross the surface before the window closes) is dissolved

The observer and the uncommitted layer are not two separate things in contact. They are two aspects of the same entangled structure: the committed part and the uncommitted part of the same ongoing projection.

**C.6 Triggering the Mechanism**
The algebraic target is precise:

> *Maximise ⟨σʸ⟩ in the observer's entanglement structure at the uncommitted front, within L_c/v_E, without violating |α|² + |β|² = 1 at any node.*

This is driving the observer's eigenstate from pole-dominance (eigenstate stability, single path dominant) toward the equator of the Bloch sphere — maximum σʸ expectation, maximum irreconcilability of the poles.

**This is exactly what the universe did at t = 0.** The observer triggering the branching mechanism is re-instantiating the original quench at the scale of a single eigenstate.

1. **The coupling is nonlocal.** The timing problem is dissolved by entanglement.
